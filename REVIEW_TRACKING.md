## Quality Gate Criteria

**Approval requires ALL of the following:**

- [ ] **Clinical_Accuracy_Agent:** Zero critical medical errors across all 45 files
  - No incorrect drug dosages
  - No incorrect emergency protocols
  - No outdated clinical guidelines
  - All differential diagnoses clinically sound

- [ ] **Australian_Standards_Agent:** 100% Australian terminology compliance
  - Zero US terminology instances (acetaminophen, albuterol, ER, PCP)
  - All Australian spelling verified (paediatric, anaemia, anaesthesia)
  - All PBS/NDSS/eTG references current and accurate
  - All phone numbers/services Australian (13 11 26, Lifeline, Beyond Blue)

- [ ] **OSCE_Format_Agent:** 100% AMC OSCE format compliance
  - All stations meet 8-minute timing requirement
  - All content follows Dr. Aamir's 9 core principles
  - All history-taking uses differential-driven approach
  - All physical exams use appropriate frameworks (5 Ps, GALS, etc.)
