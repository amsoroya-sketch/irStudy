**Edition:** 2025 Digital Subscription
**Cost:** $385/year
**Publisher:** Therapeutic Guidelines Limited
**URL:** https://tgldcdp.tg.org.au/

**Why You Need This:**
- ‚úÖ **#1 most important resource for AMC/ICRP**
- ‚úÖ Australian treatment protocols (what Australian doctors actually use)
- ‚úÖ Evidence-based prescribing guidelines
- ‚úÖ Drug dosages, interactions, contraindications
- ‚úÖ AMC exam questions directly reference eTG protocols

**Content Coverage:**
- Antibiotic guidelines
- Cardiovascular therapeutics
- Endocrine guidelines
- Neurological therapeutics
- Respiratory therapeutics
- And 20+ more specialty areas

**Alternative Access:**
- **FREE via hospital/university:** Check if you have institutional access
- Many Australian hospitals provide staff access
- University medical students get free access

**Download Format:** PDF (if institutional access) or online platform
