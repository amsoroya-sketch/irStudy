‚ö†Ô∏è High-Yield Study Tips - Don't Miss These

  1. ALWAYS practice with TIMER - 8 minutes is strict, you MUST finish on time
  2. Learn IMG mistakes - Each station has 10 common mistakes IMGs make. These are CRITICAL to avoid.
  3. Print cheat sheets - Keep them visible during study. Review before each practice session.
  4. Focus on RED FLAGS - Life-threatening conditions (ectopic shoulder pain, syncope, chest pain) = automatic fail if missed
  5. Use marking checklists - Self-assess after each practice. Aim for ‚â•56/80 (70% pass mark)
  6. Master frameworks - "5 Ps" (dyspnoea, syncope), SOCRATES (pain), CAM (delirium), "The Big 8" (falls)
  7. Practice with partner - Have someone role-play patient using the simulated patient script
  8. Verbalise your thinking - In OSCE, you must SHOW your clinical reasoning by speaking out loud

Domain What Examiners Look For

üìö How to Use This Package

Option 1: Study Alone (Self-Directed)

  1. Start with Flashcard App - build foundational knowledge (2 weeks)
  2. Work through Mock OSCE Stations systematically - one per day (10 days)
  3. Use Cheat Sheets for rapid review before each station
  4. Time yourself strictly - 8 minutes per station
  5. Self-assess using marking checklists - aim for ‚â•56/80
  6. Review IMG Common Mistakes section after each practice
  7. Repeat weak stations until consistent 70%+ scores

Option 2: Study with Partner (Recommended)

  1. Both partners study flashcards individually (Week 1-2)
  2. Meet 3√ó per week for mock OSCE practice (Week 3-8)
  3. Role-play sessions:
    • Partner A = Candidate (timed 8 minutes)
    • Partner B = Simulated Patient (reads from patient script)
    • After 8 minutes: Partner B uses marking checklist to assess Partner A
    • Discuss performance, review IMG mistakes together
    • Swap roles, repeat with different station
  4. Target: Each partner practices all 10 stations at least 3 times
  5. Final week: Full mock exam circuit (5 stations back-to-back)

Option 3: Study Group (3-4 People)

  1. Individual flashcard study (Week 1-2)
  2. Weekly group meetings (Week 3-8):
    • Each person practices 2 stations per meeting
    • Rotate roles: Candidate, Patient, Examiner (with marking checklist), Observer
    • Group feedback after each station
    • Share tips and IMG mistakes to avoid
  3. Final 2 weeks: Full mock OSCE circuits with peer assessment
