AMC Clinical / OSCE Standards

OSCE Structure Requirements

All OSCE materials MUST follow this structure:

  1. Opening Statement - Examiner-friendly introduction (30 seconds)
  2. Focused History - Systematic approach with key questions
  3. Examination Steps - Clear, sequential physical examination
  4. Differentials - Top 3 differential diagnoses (ranked by likelihood)
  5. Investigations - Appropriate for Australian context (Medicare, PBS)
  6. Management - According to Australian guidelines (eTG, AMH)
  7. Counselling & Safety-netting - Patient communication + üö® RED FLAGs

Clinical Systems Coverage

OSCE materials MUST cover all systems:

  • Medicine: Cardiovascular, Respiratory, Gastroenterology, Neurology, Endocrine
  • Surgery: Acute abdomen, Trauma, Surgical emergencies
  • Paediatrics: Common childhood illnesses, developmental assessment, immunization
  • O&G: Pregnancy care, gynecological complaints, contraception counseling
  • Psychiatry: Mental state exam, risk assessment, common psychiatric presentations
  • Ethics: Consent, confidentiality, breaking bad news, cultural safety
