üí° Tips for Effective Scheduling

Daily Minimum (2-3 hours):

  • 2-3 history taking practices (40-60 min)
  • 1 physical examination practice (15-20 min)
  • Typing practice (30-60 min)
  • 1 SOAP note (10-15 min)

Balance Your Week:

  • Weekdays: 2-3 hours daily (work around other commitments)
  • Weekends: 3-4 hours daily (more intensive practice)
  • Total target: 15-20 hours per week

Study Smart:

  • Practice hardest tasks when energy is highest (usually mornings)
  • Alternate between different skills to avoid burnout
  • Schedule breaks every 90-120 minutes
  • Use Sunday evening to plan next week
  • Review your weekly progress tracker every Sunday
