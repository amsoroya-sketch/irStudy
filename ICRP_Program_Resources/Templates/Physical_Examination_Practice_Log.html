The 5 Ps Framework

P1: Preparation - Wash hands, gather equipment, introduce self

P2: Position - Position patient appropriately for examination

P3: Permission - Explain procedure and obtain consent

P4: Perform - Conduct systematic examination

P5: Present - Summarize findings clearly
