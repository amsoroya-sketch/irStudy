## How to Use These Materials

### Study Timeline (Recommended 8 weeks)

**Weeks 1-2: Foundation**
- Read START_HERE.html
- Review Self_Assessment_Checklist.html
- Identify weak areas
- Start with History Taking Mastery Guide

**Weeks 3-5: Specialty Deep Dive**
- Focus on your 2-3 weakest specialties
- Read relevant modules thoroughly
- Practice physical examination techniques
- Review "Common IMG Mistakes" sections

**Weeks 6-7: Mock Stations**
- Practice 3 mock OSCE stations
- Time yourself (8 minutes strict)
- Record yourself (audio/video)
- Self-critique using marking rubrics

**Week 8: Consolidation**
- Review all RED FLAG warnings
- Revisit Emergency Management modules
- Practice Communication Skills scenarios
- Final mock station practice

### Daily Study Approach
- **Morning (2 hours):** Deep content review (1 module)
- **Afternoon (1 hour):** Practice physical examination technique
- **Evening (1 hour):** Mock station practice or revision
